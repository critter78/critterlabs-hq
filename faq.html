FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things people ask before they book a discovery call. Don't see yours? Ask us directly →

My broker doesn't have an API — can you still build this?
Often, yes. If your platform offers an API or OAuth, it's a clean build. If it only does CSV or statement exports, we can run scheduled imports — slightly more maintenance, but workable. If a source is fully locked (no API, no export), we'll tell you straight and either scope it as manual entry or recommend an alternative. That's exactly what the discovery call is for: we map every source before quoting.
Is this financial or investment advice?
No. We build information-aggregation and visualization tools. Your HQ shows you your own data, scored and surfaced — it doesn't tell you what to buy or sell, and we're not licensed advisors. You make the decisions; we make them easier to see.
What does the monthly fee actually cover?
Hosting, your live data feeds, monitoring, ongoing maintenance, and the AI inference that powers your daily brief and alerts. Upstream APIs change and break over time — the monthly is what keeps your HQ live, accurate, and current. It's integral to the product, not an optional add-on.
Do I own my dashboard and my data?
Your data is yours — you can export it at any time. The HQ is built and hosted by us as part of the service; if you ever leave, you keep your exported data and we part ways cleanly with 30 days' notice.
How long does a build take?
Typically 1–2 weeks for Entry, 2–4 weeks for Pro, and 4–8 weeks for All-Star — depending on how many data sources are involved and how clean they are. We confirm a timeline in your proposal before any work starts.
What if I want changes after launch?
Every build includes a revision round during the review phase. Pro includes a quarterly iteration pass and All-Star includes ongoing iteration. Larger new features outside the original scope are quoted separately — but small tweaks are part of the relationship.
Which package is right for me?
If you want one clean dashboard with a daily brief, start with Entry. If you need several feeds, custom layout, and alerts, that's Pro. If you want a full cockpit with custom AI agents and deep integrations, that's All-Star. Not sure? Book the discovery call and we'll recommend one.
Can you integrate with [my specific tool]?
If it has an API or a reliable export, almost certainly. We've wired up broker feeds, market-data providers, Polymarket, Search Console/Analytics, spreadsheets, and more. Tell us your stack on the discovery call and we'll confirm feasibility before you commit.
What's the $250 discovery call?
A focused 20-minute call where we map your data sources, confirm what's feasible, and recommend a package. It filters out guesswork — and the $250 is credited toward your build if you proceed.
Do you only build trading dashboards?
No — that's our home turf, but the same approach works anywhere you need your world on one screen. We've built an SEO operations cockpit with a custom AI marketing team and a private co-parenting family HQ using the same architecture. If it runs on data, we can build the HQ for it.
